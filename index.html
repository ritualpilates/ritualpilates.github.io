Welcome

Ritual Pilates is more than a workout – it's time for yourself. A moment of calm in your week to breathe, to move with intention, and to reconnect. Whether you're strengthening your core, improving posture, or simply carving out space for stillness, each session is your personal pause. This is fitness without pressure, movement without judgement – a ritual made just for you.

My Classes

I offer small, welcoming group sessions and 1:1 classes tailored to your needs. My daytime classes are designed with mums and older adults in mind, while my evening sessions are perfect for unwinding and finding space for yourself.

About Me

I first discovered Pilates before having children, after trying nearly every fitness style out there. But it was after becoming a mum that I truly reconnected with the practice. It helped me rebuild strength, improve posture, and reclaim something deeply needed: time for myself. That same balance of calm, focus and subtle strength is what I now share in every class.
